MEET COACH KHI

"My job isn't to make you practice. My job is to make you better. We train for results."

I'm Khi'el Alexander, also known as Coach Khi — a former collegiate guard at Concordia University who turned a self-driven basketball journey into a full-time mission to develop players. Since 2020, I've been focused on building complete, confident hoopers through structured, game-realistic training.

At PBM Elite (Powerful Beyond Measure), I work with players of all ages and positions, emphasizing skill refinement, on-court IQ, athletic performance, and a winning mindset that carries off the floor as well.

  • Walk-on collegiate experience at Concordia University, shaped by diverse coaching styles and advanced training methods.
  • Proven track record training AAU athletes and players of all levels, with a focus on comprehensive skill development and game impact.
  • Training built to unlock your full potential — elevating confidence, discipline, and Powerful Beyond Measure performance.
